Abstract

The car is a vehicle that is needed at this time. Many users when they want to choose a car only know some of the information on the car they like without knowing other similar car information. The recommended car selection system is a system that can be used by users in choosing a car. With the implementation of the recommended car selection system, users will get more information about the car they want to choose, and other cars that the user may not know about at all. In the recommendation of the car selection system, the author applies the K-Nearest Neighbor (KNN) Collaborative Filtering method which is carried out based on the proximity of the Testing Data to the Training Data. The proximity of the data (similarity of data) is used to recommend cars to users. The results obtained in this study are that if you want to get the 10 best cars, the maximum distance used is 5%, and the best accuracy is obtained when K = 10, which is 95.15%.
